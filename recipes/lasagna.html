Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known for its rich layers of flavor and comforting appeal. It features broad, flat pasta sheets alternately layered with a savory mixture of seasoned ground meat, typically beef or sausage, and a robust tomato sauce infused with garlic, onions, and aromatic herbs. Each layer is complemented by creamy ricotta cheese, gooey mozzarella, and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan, all baked to bubbly perfection. This hearty, multi-layered casserole is often finished with a golden, crispy top, making it a favorite for family dinners and special occasions alike.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
